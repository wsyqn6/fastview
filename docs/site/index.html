Why FastView?

⚡

Lightning Speed

Startup in under 1 second with instant image loading. Optimized for performance with minimal resource usage.

💾

Tiny Footprint

Only ~5MB executable size and less than 20MB memory usage. Lightweight without compromising features.

🖼️

Wide Format Support

Support JPEG, PNG, GIF, WebP, BMP, TIFF, ICO, AVIF and more. View any image format seamlessly.

🔄

EXIF Auto-Rotation

Automatically correct photo orientation based on EXIF metadata. No more sideways photos.

🎯

Smart Zoom & Pan

Fit to window, fill screen, original size, or custom zoom. Smooth drag-to-pan with keyboard shortcuts.

🌐

Cross-Platform

Native experience on Windows, Linux, and macOS. One codebase, perfect performance everywhere.
